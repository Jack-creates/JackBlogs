I miss the beach

I love going to the beach. It's one of the few things I do miss about living in Los Angeles - beaches are huge there, and in my view much prettier, than up here in Berkeley. But since this shutdown, I can't even enjoy the ones up here anymore. I find it frustrating when I hear about people going to the beach in the middle of a pandemic when the beaches are supposed to be closed! The more people disregard public health rules, the longer I'll have to wait to go the beach, because this virus won't be going anywhere until then.
